Facebook Data Deletion Instructions

If you want to delete your data associated with this app:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account Settings & Privacy.
  2. Go to Settings > Apps and Websites.
  3. Find and select this app.
  4. Click "Remove" to disconnect and delete any linked data.
  5. For further assistance, contact jeremy.coker@btinternet.com.
Note: This app does not store personal user data beyond what's needed for trend analysis, and removal from Facebook will clear everything on our end.
